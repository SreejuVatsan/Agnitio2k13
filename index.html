    Paper Presentation

    “ Speak clearly, if you speak at all; carve every word before you let it fall. ”

    - Rick Jobastans

    Description

    Tickle your ideas now! Here it is the technical paper presentation event for those who never found the right forum to stage their conceptions, mark their innovations and see the ideas transform into reality. All you need is some technical flair but a simple spark and the right forum to exhibit them. So prepare yourself to unleash the penned down thoughts in this battle for the best.

    Let your confidence reflect your contentedness!!!

    Topics:

  • Cloud Computing
  • Networking and Communication
  • Mobile Computing
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Gesture recognition
  • Cyber crime and security
  • And recent trends in Information Technology

    • Due importance is attached to uniqueness, novel and innovative ideas.

    Rules

  • Maximum two members per team can participate.
  • Abstract and full paper should be mailed to prozilos@act.edu.in. The intimation will be on 15th March 2013
  • Project demo will be displayed in front of the juries and participants will elaborate the concepts.

    • Stage I: Online paper submission

    Papers must be mailed to prozilos@act.edu.in with 'subject' specifying about the topic of the paper and should be in 'IEEE' format.

  • Send your paper on or before 12th March 2013
  • The paper should be submitted as an MS Word document (.doc format only) and papers are selected based on criteria such as effectiveness of solution proposed and innovative nature.
  • Only those participants, who mailed abstract, fullpaper and comply with the rules specified for this event, will receive an acknowledgement through mail and call.
  • The names of participants, short listed for the final round, will be displayed on the Agnitio's website & Facebook page on 15th March 2013.

    • Stage II: On-Stage Presentation During Agnitio 2k13

  • This final round will be the main on-stage event during Agntio 2k13.
  • Three hard copies of the paper and PPT and one soft copy of the same are to be submitted before presentation committee.
  • The participants are required to present their paper, in person, during the event. It is not necessary that the participants forming a team should be from the same college.
  • The participants will have to present their paper in Microsoft PowerPoint format.
  • The duration for presenting paper will be maximum of 6-8mins and will be followed by a question and answer session.
  • The decision of the judges would be final and binding.
